Nutri Cocopeat

Nutri Cocopeat is a practical planting media product from coconut fiber, which is produced with other ingredients as additional natural nutrients, so that it can nourish plants.

Our product is a new innovation breakthrough in the world of agriculture, by providing a solution to several problems with increasingly damaged soil fertility. And also as a practical medium in gardening.
